Sole Sweep Description You play as Sole a mechanic working for the Tecrua company and are tasked with repairing the atmosphere for an alien race.
Sole sweep was created for the Women in Games 2021 Jam, it was developed as part of a team including myself. The other team members are; Reyn Reynolds - Artist, Daniel Roberts - Programmer, Jessica Murray - Game Designer, and Eleanor Rogers - Narrative Designer. .
